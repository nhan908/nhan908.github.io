Shoot Schedule

Two units, one rhythm, no dead air.

The plan below keeps a camera live at nearly all times while the floating crew member handles setup, product continuity, props, resets, and talent readiness.

Global Call + Readiness

All talent arrive. HMU, wardrobe, set dressing, final product prep.

  • Team A: lighting and camera lock for Solenne
  • Team B: lighting and camera lock for Rhea
  • Crew Focus: Andrew floats on continuity, props, and readying next looks
9:45-10:20

First Hero Wave

Lock the strongest solo concepts first while energy and skin are freshest.

  • Team A: Solenne, Morning ritual, premium version
  • Team B: Rhea, First time trying the tablet
  • Crew Focus: Nick leads Team A, Amul leads Team B, Andrew captures inserts and resets

  • Crew Focus: full reset into Becky and Ben setups
10:30-11:05

Second Hero Wave

Shift into polished lifestyle and portable-use storytelling.

  • Team A: Becky, Skincare for your teeth
  • Team B: Ben, Travel / bag reveal
  • Crew Focus: Andrew handles bag props, shelf styling, and wardrobe corrections

Reset + Precision Pickups

Grab the missing beauty and travel details before pair scenes begin.

  • Team A: Becky shelfie, mirror, product hold, alt close

Final Pickup Sweep
