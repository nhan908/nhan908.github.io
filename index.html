1. Morning Ride & Arrival

10:00 AM - 11:30 AM

Depart from Stepney Green and enjoy the ride through the Essex countryside. Park at the free main car park (CM11 1WT) and head to the Visitor Centre to get your bearings.

2. The Hike: Woodland & Waterside

11:30 AM - 2:30 PM

Begin by immersing yourself in Well Wood and Hawk's Wood. Continue towards the reservoir, visiting bird hides like Lyster Hide. Extend your walk on public footpaths around the reservoir for varied scenery before returning.

Tip: Use a mapping app to create a loop back to the car park. Binoculars are highly recommended for birdwatching!

Refreshment Hub

After a good hike, you'll need a satisfying meal. Here are your options laid out side-by-side. Compare the vibe, menu highlights, and location of each place to decide where you'll stop for your post-hike break.

Cafe on the Water

Hanningfield Reservoir, CM11 1WT

Vibe: Lovely cafe with panoramic reservoir views. Perfect for a relaxing post-hike stop.

Menu Highlights:

  • Full English Breakfast
  • Jacket Potatoes & Soups
  • Fish & Chips / Pies

The Old Windmill

South Hanningfield, CM3 8HT

Vibe: Traditional, highly-rated country pub with a welcoming atmosphere.

Menu Highlights:

  • Steak & Ale Pie
  • Classic Fish & Chips
  • Good selection of real ales

The Ship

West Hanningfield, CM2 8LF

Vibe: Charming and historic pub known for friendly service and quality food.

Menu Highlights:

  • Gammon Steak or Scampi
  • Specials Board
  • Well-stocked bar
